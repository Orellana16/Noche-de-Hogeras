Epic and cinematic pop ballad. Piano-driven, in the style of Pablo Albor4n and Benson Bone. Male vocals with an extreme dynamic range: from intimate whispers and emotional falsetto to powerful, melodic screams in the choruses. The song must have a massive crescendo, starting minimalist and exploding into a cathartic chorus with powerful percussion and soaring string arrangements. Modern and polished production.

[Intro]
Náufrago en tierra de nadie,
a un universo de la orilla.
De repente, todo arde,
pues lo aguardan tus caricias.

[Verse 1]
De todos los extraños que he sido,
me quedo con el hombre en tu reflejo.
Me devuelves sin querer los latidos,
y haces que se detenga mi tiempo.

yo, que antes era un reino independiente.
